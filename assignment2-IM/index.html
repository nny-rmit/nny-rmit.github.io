who TF is m1n1 ?

m1n1 is a Melbourne-based producer for underground beats, inspired by the works of 2slimey, bleeod, and Edward Skeletrix. Currently working in the field and dropping EPs on Soundcloud, m1n1 is up-and-coming in the Melbourne underground scene, and has numerous collaborations with several other underground prods.
